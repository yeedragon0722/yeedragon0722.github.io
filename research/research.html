Areas of Interest

I am interested in AI safety, particularly in techniques ensuring the robustness and reliability of AI systems. I am a part of the Wisconsin AI Safety Initiative (WAISI).

Past Work

Previously, I have worked on Domain Adaptation and Open-set Detection with GIS images, focusing on how AI models generalize to unseen environments.

Current Focus

Currently, I am exploring the inner workings of Reinforcement Learning trained models like GPT-o1 and Deepseek R1 and how it impacts it's few-shot learning capabilities.
